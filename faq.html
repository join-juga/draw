FAQ

Answers to common questions about Drawing Game and Drawing Game.

Is it mobile-friendly?

Yes. The drawing canvas is fully responsive and works with both mouse and touch, so you can draw on your phone, tablet, or desktop.

How do I save my drawing?

Simply click the "Save" button. Your artwork will be downloaded as a PNG image file directly to your device.

How do I clear the canvas?

Click the "Clear" button to erase everything and start with a fresh canvas.

What tools are available?

You can use the color picker to choose any color you like. We plan to add more tools like different brush sizes in the future!

Is my art private?

Yes. Your drawings are not uploaded to any server. When you click "Save," the image is created and saved only on your own computer or device.
